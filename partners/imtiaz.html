Building Strong Alliances with Leading Developers in Dubai

At AIR One Real Estate LLC SOC, we collaborate with some of the most reputable and established real estate developers in Dubai. Our partnerships allow us to provide clients with direct access to premium projects, priority inventory, and verified investment opportunities.

Imtiaz Developments is a private real estate development company founded in 1993, headquartered in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. The developer is recognised for delivering residential and mixed use properties that combine contemporary design with lifestyle focused amenities. Its portfolio primarily serves homebuyers, expatriates, and investors seeking quality living environments and sustainable returns. Project types include apartments, villas, townhouses, waterfront residences, and mixed use communities in strategic locations across Dubai.

Landmark Projects

Pearl House (Jumeirah Village Circle)

A series of fully furnished residential buildings with studios and apartments, known for early handover performance and smart home features.

Westwood Grande (Al Furjan / JVC)

Premium residential community offering contemporary living, landscaped amenities, and strong rental appeal.

Beach Walk 4 (Dubai Islands)

Part of a high end waterfront collection targeting luxury lifestyle living with modern design and sea views.

Wynwood Horizon (Meydan)

Emerging residential project in Meydan offering modern living, integrated lifestyle amenities, and proximity to business and entertainment hubs
