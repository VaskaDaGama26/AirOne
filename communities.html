DAMAC Islands 2

DAMAC Islands 2 is a large-scale villa community with an island-inspired concept. The project is inspired by the atmosphere of famous island destinations and combines a resort-style lifestyle with modern residential living. Each cluster will reflect the unique atmosphere of different islands around the world, including architecture, landscape design, and natural surroundings.

Emaar The Valley

The Valley is a large residential community by Emaar designed for peaceful family living outside the city center. The project combines modern architecture, expansive green spaces, and well-developed infrastructure within the community. The community is designed as a self-sufficient neighborhood with everything needed for daily life, including parks, schools, sports areas, and retail spaces.

DAMAC Lagoons

DAMAC Lagoons is a large residential community in Dubai with a resort-style concept inspired by the Mediterranean lifestyle. The project combines residential clusters, water features, and well-developed infrastructure designed for comfortable family living. Nad Al Sheba Gardens

Nad Al Sheba Gardens is a modern gated community of villas and townhouses by Meraas located close to Dubai’s central districts. SHA Residences Emirates (SHA Wellness Villas)

SHA Residences Emirates is an exclusive wellness project on the UAE coastline created in collaboration with the international health brand SHA Wellness Clinic.
