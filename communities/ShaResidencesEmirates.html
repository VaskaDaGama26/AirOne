Sha Residences Emirates

SHA Residences Emirates is an exclusive wellness project on the UAE coastline created in collaboration with the international health brand SHA Wellness Clinic. The project combines luxury residences, natural surroundings, and world-class wellness infrastructure to create a unique community for living and wellbeing. The community is located in the natural area of Al Jurf between Dubai and Abu Dhabi and offers privacy, beaches, and green spaces.
